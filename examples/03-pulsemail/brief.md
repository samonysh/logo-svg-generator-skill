# PulseMail — Focus-friendly email client

Design a logo for **PulseMail**, a focus-friendly email client that batches notifications into three daily "pulses" so users check email deliberately, not reactively.

- **Audience:** knowledge workers, remote teams
- **Vibe:** warm, calm, alive, human
- **Avoid:** corporate, cold, over-technical, sterile
- **Preferred color:** coral `#EF4444`
- **Deliverables:** monochrome master + branded variant, standard multi-size set
- **Metaphor hints:** classic envelope silhouette with a heartbeat / pulse waveform crossing the flap

Follow Simple Icons rules (single `<path>`, no fill on master, touches ≥ 2 sides of the 24×24 viewBox).
